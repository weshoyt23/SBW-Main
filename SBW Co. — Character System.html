character system — living archive
Three rodeo clowns. Wacky and wild on the surface — their entire purpose is to protect you and pick you up when you're down. In a real rodeo, clowns distract the bull and make the crowd laugh so the fear doesn't become unbearable. Nameless, gender ambiguous archetypes — anyone can see themselves or someone they love in them. Unified illustration style: heavy confident ink outlines, painted color fill with ink shading, weathered patched fabric texture, grounded warm human expression. Pose, palette, and costume detail are what separate each character — not the rendering technique.
